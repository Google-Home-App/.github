## Setup Help and Common Fixes

- Device not appearing in Google Home? Confirm the device is powered on, connected to the same Wi-Fi network, and ready for google home setup.  
- google home app cannot link a service? Check account permissions, update the app, and retry linking from the device or service settings.  
- google home speaker or google home mini not responding? Restart the device, verify microphone settings, and confirm the correct home and room assignment.  
- google home routines are not running? Review trigger conditions, household presence settings, device names, and google home automation permissions.  
- google home wifi or connected devices feel unstable? Restart the router, check signal strength, and move key devices closer to the network source.  
- Need more guidance? Use google home support inside the app for device-specific steps, account help, and compatibility information.
